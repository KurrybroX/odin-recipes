Thai Coconut Soup

Description

Better than the soup nazi's soup

Wow guests and friends with this ancient thai secret

Ingredients

  • Thai Coconut Broth
  • 2 Cans of coconut milk
  • 2 limes
  • 1 twig of lemon grass, sliced and crushed
  • Ginger paste
  • 1/2 of a medium red onion
  • Frozen pre-cooked Shrimp
  • crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 pouch of microwave rice

Steps

  1. Oil large pot and saute onions, red pepper, ginger, and lemon grass for roughly 5 minutes
  2. Add broth and coconut milk; bring to a boil
  3. Cut and squeeze limes in to the broth; drop in as well
  4. Reduce heat and add shrimp
  5. Heat rice
  6. Spoon out lemograss and limes
  7. Spoon out shrimp and onion on to rice
  8. Pour desired amount of broth over rice
