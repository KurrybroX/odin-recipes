Lasagna

Description

Like the cool cat Garfield; Lasagna has always been one of my personal faves. This dish will surely add layers to your gut and repetoire.

While it's definitely not one of the more complicated dishes, it will take a bit of time to prepare and does have many steps to the process.

Ingredients

  • Dry Lasagna pasta
  • 16 oz container of Ricotta
  • 4 cups Mozzarella
  • 1/2 lb ground Beef and Sausage
  • 2 Jars of boticelli's Garlic and Herb Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil and cool Lasagna pasta
  2. Brown meat in pan, drain
  3. Line bottom of lasagna dish with a layer of the pasta
  4. Apply a layer of meat and mozzarella. Top with another layer of pasta
  5. Apply a layer of ricotta and tomato sauce. Top with another layer of pasta
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until all ingredients are used up or until you are at the top of your dish
  7. Apply the remaining amount of mozzarella on top dish, drizzle with preffered oil, and cover in foil
  8. Bake in preheated oven at 375 degrees F for 25-30 minutes until mozzarella is browned
